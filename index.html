About Swaple.io

Swaple is cryptocurrency swapping platform. Swaple enables novice and veteran investors alike to benefit from zero service fees, limitless coin and token options, higher levels of privacy, and much more. The Swaple Platform is beneficial to all, but is designed to welcome a new generation of cryptocurrency users to the market in order to stimulate greater adoption and proliferation of cryptocurrencies.

                FAQ

                Frequently Asked Questions

                • What is Swaple?

                  Swaple is cryptocurrency swapping platform where digital assets can be conveniently, quickly, safely and privately swapped. The Swaple Platform does not have charts or technical analysis tools but instead features a more comfortable and accessible bulletin board interface. People who are not experienced in technical analysis or have no need for such tools will find it much more user-friendly. Swaple Platform is a place where both experienced and novice investors alike can comfortably maneuver within the local and global cryptocurrency marketplace.

                • How do I find a swap?

                  Swappers wanting to find peers to swap with will be able to do so using the Swaple Platform’s Bulletin Board. Here swappers can either post the details of their desired swaps or browse already posted swaps to find another swapper to swap with.

                • What coins can I swap?

                  Any coin or token that two swappers want to swap can be swapped. The only requirement for a coin or token to be swapped on the Swaple Platform is two agreeable swappers.

                • How does a swap work?

                  Once two swappers have found each other via the bulletin board and have agreed to a swap, they will then proceed to the swapping interface. Each party will be prompted to send the specified amount of their coin to the escrow wallets provided them. They will then be asked for their receiving wallet address.

                  Once both parties have sent their asset to the escrow and the transactions have been confirmed, the assets will then proceed on to the respective wallets provided by the swappers.

                  Swappers will receive the exact amount specified in the original bulletin board post, and their rates will not increase unexpectedly based on the volume being swapped or any other factors because a traditional exchange is never used and the rates are agreed upon by both swappers ahead of time.

                • How does a pool swap work?

                  On the swapping interface page is the option to create a swapping pool. A swapping pool gives swappers the ability to combine their funds to meet the swapping volume needs of the various swaps on the bulletin board. Any swapper may start a pool. When enough swappers have joined the pool and the required asset amount is achieved, the swap will be facilitated.

                • Are there limitations on the size of swaps?

                  Swaple Platform does not put any limits on the amount a swapper can swap. A swap can be as big or small as the swappers involved can mutually agree on.

                • What are the fees for swapping?

                  Swaple Platform is a swapping platform, and so swappers using SWAP Coin to facilitate a swap are not charged any service fees for using the platform. The amount of SWAP needed to facilitate a swap is the equivalent of 0.1% of the volume being swapped. All the SWAP Coin used each month to facilitate swaps will be returned to swappers’ respective wallets at the end of each month, making swapping free. Swappers do still have the option to facilitate swaps by paying a fee in altcoins or bitcoin at the rate of 0.1% of the volume being swapped, if they feel inclined to do so.

                  Facilitating a pool swap will be the same as facilitating a regular swap. The only difference will be that the SWAP Coins, altcoins, or bitcoin needed to facilitate a swap will be split equally among the swappers in the swapping pool.

                • How do I make a swapping pool?

                  When a swapper is creating or responding to a post on the bulletin board, they will be given the option to create an individual or pool swap. After selecting the pool swap option, a swapper can set the parameters of the swap they want other swappers to join.

                • Do I have to make a swapper account?

                  Swaple Platform does not require swappers to make a swapper account. There is the option to use the guest account, but those swappers who do choose to make a swapper account will get the extra benefit of swapper ratings and swap history. Swapper ratings and swap history will help swappers find more reliable swappers to swap with. As an added benefit, those swappers with exceptional swapper-ratings will receive a discount on their swapping fees.

                • Do I hold any funds on the Swaple Platform?

                  The Swaple Platform is different in that no funds are actually stored on the platform. All funds stay in the swappers’ own wallets until a swap is initiated. The swapper is in complete control of their funds until the moment they send it to be swapped.
